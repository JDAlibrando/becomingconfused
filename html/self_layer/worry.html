Without Worry

What would I be without worry?
I worry that, without worry, I would not get anything done.
I worry that, without worry, I will let myself be wronged.
I worry that, without worry, I would miss opportunities.
I worry that, without worry, I will wrong others.

What would I be without worry?
I don't know, but I wouldn't worry about it.
